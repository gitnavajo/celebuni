CelebUni is a minimalist **celebrity fan directory** designed around a fast search flow:

- A big, centered search bar
- Quick category tabs (Actors, Voice Actors, Musicians)
- A results page with a short bio, key credits (TMDb), and placeholders for conventions + fan-mail

This blog section is powered by markdown files stored locally in the repo for now.
